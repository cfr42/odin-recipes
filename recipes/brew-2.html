Brew II: Confectio Alchermes

Prepare 6-8 small glass jars by sterilising for 5-10 minutes in boiling water.

Ingredients

  • Unicorn horn1
  • Ambergris
  • Cinnamon
  • Aloes
  • Gold leaf
  • Musk
  • Lapis lazuli, pulverized if possible
  • White pearls

Instructions

  1. Grind the unicorn horn.
  2. Pulverise lapis lazuli, if required.
  3. Chop aloe leaves, removing tough parts, then mash to a fine paste.
  4. Grind cinnamon.
  5. Combine horn, lapis and cinnamon.
  6. Grind gold leaf and white pearls to a fine powder, then add.
  7. Mix thoroughly before adding musk and ambergris.
  8. Mix again to form a smooth paste.
  9. Portion into jars.
  10. Store in a cool, dark place.

1If you have difficulty obtaining unicorn horn, narwhal horn makes an adequate, if not entirely satisfactory, substitute.
